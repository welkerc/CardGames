🎰 Video Poker Rules

Objective: Build the best poker hand possible to win credits!

Variants

  • Jacks or Better: Need at least a pair of Jacks to win. Classic 9/6 paytable (99.54% RTP).
  • Deuces Wild: All 2s are wild! Need three of a kind or better to win. Higher variance.

How to Play

  • Bet: Choose your bet (1-5 credits) using Bet 1, Max Bet, Half, or Double
  • Deal: Press Deal to receive 5 cards
  • Hold: Click cards you want to keep (they become highlighted)
  • Draw: Press Draw to replace unheld cards
  • Payout: Winning hands are paid according to the paytable
  • Max Bet Bonus: Bet 5 credits on a Royal Flush for 800x payout!

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Press 1-5 to toggle hold on cards
  • Press D or Space to Deal/Draw
